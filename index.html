Preprint

Optimal Average Success Probabilities of Binary (n, n−1) and (n, n−2) Quantum Random Access Codes via a Proof of the Corresponding Conjectured Bound

Shuo Tan, Syed A. Jafar · arXiv:2607.10414 · quant-ph, cs.IT

We prove the conjectured upper bound on the optimal average success probability of binary quantum random access codes for m = n − 1 and m = n − 2.

Contribution. This exactly characterizes both QRAC families and certifies the optimality of recent constructions, using a local-to-global reconstruction argument via the pretty good measurement together with dimension and positive-semidefinite constraints on an induced channel.

Preprint

2024

Flexible Coded Distributed Convolution Computing for Enhanced Straggler Resilience and Numerical Stability in Distributed CNNs

Shuo Tan, Rui Liu, Xuesong Han, Xianlei Long, Kai Wan, Linqi Song, Yong Li · arXiv:2411.01579 · cs.DC

We introduce the Flexible Coded Distributed Convolution Computing framework, extending circulant and rotation-matrix embedding from matrix multiplication to high-dimensional tensor convolution.

Contribution. New coded partitioning strategies enable a communication–storage tradeoff while improving straggler resilience, numerical stability, and scalability across distributed CNN architectures.
